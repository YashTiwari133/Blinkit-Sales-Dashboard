# 📊 Blinkit Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes Blinkit's grocery sales performance by providing actionable insights into sales trends, outlet performance, customer ratings, and product categories. It enables businesses to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and make data-driven decisions through interactive visualizations.

## 💼 Business Problem

Blinkit requires a centralized analytics dashboard to evaluate sales performance across different outlet locations, outlet sizes, and product categories. The objective is to identify high-performing outlets, understand customer preferences, and uncover business opportunities for improved operational efficiency and strategic decision-making.

## 🎯 Key Objectives

- Analyze Blinkit's overall sales performance.
- Compare sales across different outlet types and outlet sizes.
- Identify top-selling product categories.
- Evaluate customer ratings and average sales.
- Track sales trends based on outlet establishment year.
- Understand the impact of outlet locations on revenue generation.
- Provide interactive filtering for detailed business analysis.
- 
## 📈 Dashboard Features

- KPI Cards (Total Sales, Average Sales, Total Items, Average Rating)
- Sales trend analysis by outlet establishment year
- Outlet size performance analysis
- Outlet location comparison (Tier 1, Tier 2 & Tier 3)
- Product category (Item Type) sales analysis
- Fat content distribution analysis
- Outlet type performance comparison
- Interactive slicers for Outlet Location Type, Outlet Size, and Item Type

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- Power BI Desktop
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Data Modeling
- Microsoft Excel

## 📌 Key Insights

- Achieved **$1.20M** in total sales across all outlets.
- Sold over **8,500** grocery items with an average sale value of **$141**.
- Maintained an average customer rating of **3.9**, indicating positive customer satisfaction.
- **Tier 3 outlets** generated the highest sales among all outlet locations.
- **Medium-sized outlets** contributed the largest share of revenue.
- **Supermarket Type 1** emerged as the best-performing outlet type.
- **Fruits & Vegetables** and **Snack Foods** were the highest-selling product categories.
- Sales peaked for outlets established around **2018**, indicating stronger performance from newer outlets.
- Regular fat products generated higher sales compared to low-fat products.
